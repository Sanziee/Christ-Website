1+3 Student Transfer Program
with THWS,Germany



The first-year students (2nd Semester) studying at
School of Engineering and Technology may apply to
study rest of three years of engineering at THWS,
Germany.

2+2 KNU South Korea
Transfer Program



The 2nd Year students (4th Semester) of Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering and Department of Computer Science and Engineering may apply to study rest of the two years of engineering at Kyungpook National University (KNU), South Korea.

2+2 UNSW-Sydney, Australia Transfer Program



The 2nd Year students (4th Semester) of Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering may apply to study rest of the two years of engineering at University of New South Wales (UNSW), Sydney, Australia.

Semester Exchange
Programs



Students of School of Engineering and Technology (SoET), may apply for semester exchange program wherein they may study partnered university for one semester.

International Project / Internship / Immersion Program



Students of School of Engineering and Technology (SoET), may apply for short term program wherein they may carry out their project work or internship at partnered university about two weeks to one month.
